The Greatest Commandment in Computer Ethics

In 1992, the 10 Commandments of Computer Ethics was released by the Computer Ethics Institute that is first presented by Dr. Ramon C. Barquin on his paper. This is somehow complex for an average computer user to memorize and understand.
That's why TheDoggyBrad Software Labs formulated the "Greatest Commandment" using the existing 10 Commandments of Computer Ethics.




"You should use your computer in ways that respect and show consideration to other people and will not cause harm or damage to them in any form."



Reference:
Barquin, Ramon C. (1992). Ten Commandments of Computer Ethics. Computer Ethics Institute - 10 Commandments of Computer Ethics. https://computerethicsinstitute.org/publications/tencommandments.html
